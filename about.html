A bit about me!

Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I'm a firm believer in the philosophy that growth comes from pushing your boundaries, whether it's conquering mountains, mastering new skills, or setting personal records on the bike - I thrive on challenges and the pursuit of excellence.

My journey began with a passion for teaching English literature, driven by a desire to share my love for literature with others. As I delved deeper into academia, my enthusiasm for lifelong learning grew, shaping my approach to both personal and professional development.

I moved into the tech industry during the crash, starting from the ground up as a call center agent. Through dedication and perseverance, I moved up though various CX departments, ultimately landing the role of Head of Customer Experience for one of Ireland's trusted brands. Working with global giants like BMW, and launching BMW Financial Services' first global NPS programme, provided invaluable insights into brand management, corporate governance, and the intricacies of customer experience.

Today, I continue to embrace every opportunity for growth and innovation, fueled by a relentless passion for excellence. I'm excited to share my journey and expertise and help to disrupt an industry long overdue for disruption."
